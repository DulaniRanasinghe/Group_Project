About Us

The Olive Garden name is proudly borne by a growing collection of distinguished properties around the world. we are committed to providing our guests with memorable journeys inspired by exquisite European flair. We believe life should be lived with style.Each year, an increasing number of guests come to appreciate these qualities. While this growth reflects the strength and success of the Olive Garden brand, the collection will remain a limited one, where exclusivity can be nurtured and individuality can flourish.
